Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Multi Family Trip Tracker (the "Service"), operated by triptracking.org ("we", "us"), collects and how it's used. The Service is invite-only and not open to the general public.

1. What we collect

Account info: your name and email address, provided by whichever sign-in method you choose (Microsoft, Google, or Yahoo). We never see or store your password — sign-in is handled entirely by that provider.

Content you create: trips, dates, notes, itineraries, comments, gallery photos, and attachments (tickets/confirmations) you or your family upload.

Usage data: basic sign-in timestamps and "who's online" presence, used only to show activity and login stats to family/site admins.

2. How we use it

Solely to operate the Service for you: displaying your trips and your family's trips, enforcing who can see or edit what, sending courtesy notification emails (invites, family shares, access approvals), and showing activity/presence to admins. We do not sell data or use it for advertising.

3. Where it's stored

Trips, photos, itineraries, comments, and attachments are stored in Azure Blob Storage under our Azure subscription. Notification emails are sent via Resend. Local browser storage (localStorage) is used for display preferences (theme, "seen" markers) and never synced elsewhere.

4. Who can see your data

Trips are private to your family by default. You control whether and how your family's trips are shared with other invited families, down to marking individual trips private even when broader sharing is on. Site administrators can access data for support and moderation purposes.

5. Cookies & sign-in

Sign-in uses your identity provider's standard authentication cookies to keep you signed in. We don't use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your choices

You can delete your own comments, gallery photos, and attachments at any time. You can request deletion of your account and associated trips by contacting us below, or use the in-app "Delete my account" option if available to you.

7. Changes

We may update this policy occasionally; continued use of the Service after an update means you accept the change.
